None of them can die

Master the art of multi-character control in this intense parkour challenge where you must keep all stickmen alive while navigating through endless obstacles.

Game Features

  • Control up to 5 stickmen simultaneously
  • Multiple parallel tracks with unique obstacles
  • Increasing difficulty as you progress
  • Real-time reaction and coordination challenges
  • Endless gameplay with score tracking

How to Play

Use mouse left controls to manage multiple stickmen running on different tracks. Each stickman must avoid obstacles independently. The game ends when any stickman collides with an obstacle. Your goal is to achieve the highest possible score by keeping all stickmen alive for as long as possible.

Tips & Strategies

Start with controlling fewer stickmen and gradually increase the number as you improve. Focus on developing a rhythm for each track and maintain awareness of all characters simultaneously. Practice regularly to improve your reaction time and coordination skills.
